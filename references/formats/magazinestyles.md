# Magazine Article Styles

## Nature (News & Views / Research Highlights)

- 800-1500 words
- Accessible to interdisciplinary scientists
- Lead with the finding, not the background
- One clear takeaway per paragraph
- No equations in body text
- Quote from independent expert

## Quanta Magazine

- 2000-5000 words
- Explanatory depth for science-curious readers
- Scientist as protagonist (profiles, journeys)
- Use analogies from everyday life
- Visual metaphors heavily used
- Timeline of discovery when relevant

## MIT Tech Review

- 1500-3000 words
- Business + technology intersection
- TR35 innovators profile format
- Market implications mandatory
- "Why this matters now" framing
- Expert quotes and contrarian views

## IEEE Spectrum

- 1500-3000 words
- Engineering depth for technical readers
- System diagrams encouraged
- Historical context of the technology
- Standards and specifications mentioned
- Industry impact analysis

## Wired

- 1500-4000 words
- Cultural angle on technology
- Narrative journalism style
- Scene-setting lead
- Human impact focus
- Bold predictions acceptable

## Usage

```bash
python scripts/wizard.py --format magazine --style nature
python scripts/wizard.py --format magazine --style quanta
```
